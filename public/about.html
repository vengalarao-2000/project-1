About Paws & Pixels

We help pet lovers turn everyday photos into ready-to-post stories. Connect Google Photos or Pinterest, and get AI-crafted captions with mood tags—plus smart auto-crop renditions (1:1, 4:5, 16:9, 9:16) that keep your pet perfectly centered.

Launch Studio

Our mission

Make it simple (and fun) for people to celebrate their pets online. Paws & Pixels uses computer vision to understand what’s in your photos and a language model to write short, on-tone captions with mood tags you can filter and export.

How it works

Connect your photos

Choose Google Photos or upload a few images to start.

We analyze each image

Vision detects what’s in the photo; Gemini drafts a caption and mood tags.

Smart auto-crop

Automatic 1:1, 4:5, 16:9, and 9:16 crops that keep your pet in focus.

Copy, filter & export

Save favorites, filter by mood, and export captions for posting.

Why it matters

  • • Faster posting: No more staring at a blank caption box.
  • • Consistent tone: Keep your page vibe steady and fun.
  • • Organization: Mood tags and labels make search simple.
  • • Export-ready: Use captions anywhere (schedulers, Sheets, Notion).
  • • Perfect framing: One-click crops for feed, reels, stories, and banners.

Privacy & data

  • • You pick the photos to analyze; nothing else is scanned.
  • • Results are stored to your account; you can delete them anytime.
  • • We log processing for transparency; your photos aren’t resold.
